The Foundery Church

Forge Defender

Protect the glowing forge. Break the negative forces. Catch blessings. Build your flame. Survive as long as you can.

Desktop: click items and press Space for Blessing Burst. Mobile: tap items and use the Burst button.

How to Play

Defend the Flame

Tap dark enemies before they hit the center forge. Tap glowing blessings to fill your Blessing Meter. When the meter is full, use Blessing Burst to clear enemies, heal the forge, and score big.

Power-Ups

Grace Shield blocks damage. Prayer Pulse slows enemies. Hope Burst clears nearby threats. Community Chain doubles points. Forge Fire burns enemies near the forge.
